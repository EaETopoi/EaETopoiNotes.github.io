Application: Motion Planning

Configuration spaces are essential for planning the motion of large numbers of bodies moving about the same space so that they don't collide. Some examples are the following:

  1. Guided robots moving about a factory
  2. Air traffic control at a busy airport
  3. Motion planning on tracks
