Introduction/Motivation

These notes are for a 30~40 minute talk on semi-additivity as appearing in Sections 2.1 and 2.2 of the paper Ambidexterity and Height[1], which was given as part of an Ambidexterity seminar at UIUC in Fall 2025. The -self maps used in the definition of chromatic height are instead replaced by the cardinalities of certain -finite spaces (to be discussed soon).
