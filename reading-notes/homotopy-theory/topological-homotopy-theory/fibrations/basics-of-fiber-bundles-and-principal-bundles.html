1. Introduction

In this note we aim to provide a basic introduction to the theory of fiber bundles, and the special role principal bundles play in their classification. These notes are primarily adapted from Rui's Math 519 course notes in Spring 2025 at UIUC, with additional perspectives from Jake Rasmussen's Math 526 in Fall 2024.
